What is an escrow service?
+
An escrow service acts as a neutral third party that holds and manages funds or assets during a transaction between two parties (e.g., buyer and seller) until the terms of the agreement are met. The escrow service ensures that these terms are met before releasing the funds to the seller, providing security and confidence to both parties.
How does the process work?
+
The escrow process typically involves the following steps:
Step 1: The buyer and seller agree on the terms of the transaction and select Credellex as their trusted escrow service provider.
Step 2: The buyer deposits the agreed-upon funds into the escrow account provided by Credellex.
Step 3: The seller delivers the goods or services as per the agreement.
Step 4: The buyer inspects the goods or services and confirms satisfaction.
Step 5: Once all terms are met, Credellex releases the funds to the seller.

+
Yes, your money is safe with Credellex. We implement industry-standard security protocols, maintain proper licensing and compliance with financial regulations, and keep all escrow funds in segregated accounts separate from our operating accounts to ensure maximum protection.

+
